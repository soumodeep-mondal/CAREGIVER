welcome to

Caregiver is a giver of care. Someone who provides support, help, or aid to another who needs it. In simple terms, caregiver is a unit who tends to the needs or concerns of a person with short-or long-term limitations due to illness, injury or disability.

On the path of recovery, post surgical care is one of the essential components. A patient requires attention to make a quick recovery. We at CAREGIVER know this, and this is why we have the best accommodation and nursing facilities available for post surgical care.

Our procedure varies from patient to patient, creating a specific chart plan, and then begin the process. We offer medical guidance in the fields listed below.

The reason why you should be encouraged to have Post surgical care at CAREGIVER is very simple. When the patient has a warm and hospitable environment, they recover much faster than being in a hospital. A friendly space keeps their mind occupied with positive thoughts, thus helping in recovery.
